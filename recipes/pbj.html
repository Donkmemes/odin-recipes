The Perfect Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

This recipe is inspired by the classic peanut butter jelly sandwich, a modern cullinary staple.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Spread a heafty serving of peanut butter onto a slice of bread using a butter knife.
  2. Spread an equal portion of jelly onto the remaining slice of bread.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together so that the peanut butter and jelly collide and form a sandwich.
  4. Cut the sandwich in half diagnoly. Enjoy!
